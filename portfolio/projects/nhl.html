Part 01 — XR Coaching App
My Role

Product Manager & Contract Owner

I joined as Product Manager with the primary objective of renewing the NHL contract — Immersiv.io's largest client relationship at the time. Assigned to the XR Coaching App on Apple Vision Pro, I defined the project scope, structured the product backlog, and established the development roadmap internally.

Through weekly C-level meetings with NHL leadership, I provided clear progress updates, addressed risks, and aligned stakeholders on next steps — directly contributing to the successful renewal and expansion of the partnership: $1.4M over two NHL seasons.

Project Overview

The NHL XR Coaching App was developed in partnership with Apple for Apple Vision Pro — delivering an immersive platform for professional hockey coaches and analysts. It enables coaching staff to review current and past season games in 3D, analyze player performance, scout recruits, and present tactical insights in a spatial environment.

Built in close collaboration with NHL teams working directly with coaches, the app was designed to support real-world elite workflows — combining XR technology with practical coaching needs to evaluate games, track player metrics, and support strategic decision-making.

Part 02 — Fan Experience
My Role

NHL Mobile XR — Fan First

I started the project with responsibility for evolving an existing mobile 3D app developed one year earlier. My primary objective was to improve the product ahead of fan testing — ensuring it was mature enough for users to evaluate core value rather than surface UX issues.

I conducted a comprehensive analysis, identified key pain points, prioritized by impact and feasibility, and presented a structured improvement plan to C-level stakeholders. This established the foundation for the first development cycle and guided subsequent improvements.

Project Overview

3D Hockey for Every Fan

Building on the XR capabilities of the Coaching App, this fan-facing experience is accessible through the official NHL mobile app. Fans follow games live or in replay through a 3D recreation of the ice — in a realistic or gamified style — exploring player-level stats in real time and engaging with matches in a more immersive, data-driven way.

Experience Overview

Core Features Across Both Platforms

🏒
Game & Team Navigation Select teams, games, and specific periods to review across mobile and Vision Pro. Past games persist across sessions, enabling efficient repeat usage for both fans and coaching staff.
⏱️
Timeline-Driven Match Exploration A fully interactive timeline with play, pause, rewind, fast-forward, and frame-by-frame navigation. Filter by events (goals, shots, faceoffs, shifts), add bookmarks, and navigate matches for both casual review and tactical analysis.
📊
Player Performance Insights Player-level insights surface directly within the 3D recreation — shifts, key events, time on ice, and contextual stats. Supports fan engagement on mobile and professional scouting and coaching workflows on Vision Pro.
🎯
Viewing Modes & Immersive Interaction Coaches can inhabit a player's perspective or toggle between strategic cameras with granular control over FOV (Field of View), hip orientation, and proximity — providing a precise behavioral dataset for elite player-coach film sessions and scouting.

Conclusion

Working across both the NHL Mobile XR experience and the Apple Vision Pro Coaching App gave me a comprehensive view of how immersive technology can serve very different audiences — fans and professional coaches — within the same ecosystem. From refining a mobile product ahead of fan testing to shaping an XR coaching tool for elite analysis, I learned to balance innovation with practicality.

Managing scope, prioritization, and decision-making under business-critical constraints — directly contributing to a $1.4M contract renewal — reinforced how clarity, alignment, and measurable outcomes drive both product success and long-term client trust.
