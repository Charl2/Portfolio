My Role

Product Manager, Immersiv.io

I joined the PSG project as Product Manager with the primary objective of delivering features within the established scope, while shaping the user experiences connected to those features. I led the ideation and design of key app surfaces — the home page, match list, and video gallery — working closely with the internal team to define concepts and present them to the client.

In parallel, I built a close working relationship with PSG's Digital Product and Marketing teams and the Google XR Director through weekly C-level meetings — providing progress updates, surfacing risks, and aligning on next steps. This collaboration was key to ensuring on-time delivery.

Project Overview

Redefining How Matches Are Followed

The PSG XR app was funded by Google to support the launch of the Android XR headset in the US and South Korea, developed in collaboration with Samsung on the hardware side. From the home page, fans access live matches and replays in both 2D and 180°, match highlights, and exclusive premium content via a MyParis Premium account — enhanced with real-time stats and a 3D tabletop view.

The key differentiator is the 180° live and replay viewing mode. With five cameras installed inside Parc des Princes, users watch matches from within the stadium — with automatic camera switching and spatial audio designed to capture the energy of a live PSG match.

Experience Overview

Four Surfaces, One App

🏠
A Home Page Guiding the Experience The left panel adapts to match status — live match, full replay, pre-match teaser or upcoming fixture. The centre highlights the latest videos; the right panel surfaces exclusive 180° content for immediate access.
📅
Match List to Track the Team's Progress Fans browse PSG's full season calendar, review past scores and stats, and access replays in 2D, 3D tabletop, or immersive 180° — revisiting matches at their own pace.
🎬
Access to Exclusive PSG Content The video gallery centralises all PSG content across current and past seasons. Users filter by match, season, or format — surfacing 180° highlights, full replays, and premium content.
📡
A New Way of Broadcasting Football Five cameras inside Parc des Princes, automatic switching, and spatial audio bring fans closer to the action — combining 2D, 3D tabletop, and 180° stadium experiences in one app.
